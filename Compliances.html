About

Sartha Micro Finance Company is a non-profit making organization incorporated under section 8 of Companies Act, 2013 in 2016 to reduce poverty, by carrying on the business of providing micro finance services, as permitted from time to time by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) exclusively to large number of poor men and women and thus to help them and their families out of the poverty, further to provide collateral free credit to poor’s and to deliver micro credit and other permitted financial services to them, with a view to provide them a sustainable livelihood above the poverty-line, and of creating financially-sustainable, community-owned micro finance institution for the poor’s.

VISION

“Bringing what underprivileged wants in their hands by providing financial assistance where customer satisfaction and the culture of excellence reign.”
